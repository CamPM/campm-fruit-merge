Detailed Instructions

Movement

Drag anywhere. A dashed line shows exactly where your fruit will fall. Release to drop.

Death Line

The Red Dotted Line is the limit. If fruits stay above it for 2s, the game resets. Your Wallet and High Score are always saved.

Shop & Power-Ups

Select a Power-Up and drag it into the field. Target fruits glow Gold. If you don't have the cash, you'll see a warning.
