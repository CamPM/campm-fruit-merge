Help & Guide

How to Play

1. Drag to aim, release to drop fruit.
2. Merge identical fruits to evolve them.
3. Don't let fruits stay above the Red Dash Line for 2 seconds!

Power-Up Dragging

Hold a power-up button and drag it into the field. Fruits that will be affected will glow Gold. Release to buy and use instantly.
